Bendy Beams

Welcome to Bendy Beams, the game of reflections!

Our hero turtle, Herman, is on a mission. The evil Dr. Ben D. Beams has imprisoned him in a maze of mirrors and lazer beams. He will not let Herman go until the brainy turtle is able to solve twenty seperate puzzles, each more difficult than the one before. Only after the final puzzle has been solved will Herman be granted refuge into the waters of the Nile River, his home.

Help Herman get back home by directing him on how to solve each puzzle!
